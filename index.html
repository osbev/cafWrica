Lost Books

Lost Books is one of those places you stumble upon and never want to leave. Hidden inside the CAO Mercado Building on Osmeña Blvd, this café-bookstore hybrid is the perfect retreat from Cebu’s bustling streets. The moment I stepped in, I felt like I had entered a different world—shelves filled with local literature, cozy nooks for reading, and the comforting aroma of freshly brewed coffee.

What makes Lost Books special isn’t just its ambiance—it’s the experience. For just ₱250 per month, you can borrow books and immerse yourself in stories beyond a single visit. I personally loved their collection of Filipiniana and Visayan literature, a refreshing find in a world where mainstream books often dominate.

  • Location: CAO Mercado Building, Osmeña Blvd, Cebu City
  • Operating Hours:
    10:00 AM – 7:00 PM
    Closed on Thursdays

Coastal Coffee

If you're heading north, Coastal Coffee in Panalipan, Catmon is a perfect stop to unwind by the sea. With its alfresco setup, pet-friendly space, and ample parking, it’s a great place to take a breather from the road.

Their cold brew, pour-over coffee, and best-selling ube latte pair well with their highly recommended carbonara (10/10, they say!).

  • Location: Along the northern highway, before Sealsalt Cafe if coming from Cebu City
  • Operating Hours:
    7 AM – 6 PM | Tue–Sun
    Closed on Mondays

Bloq Cafe

by Third Wave Coffee

Located on the 2nd floor of Bloq Res. Showroom, Third Wave Coffee is a great place to slow down—whether you're having a solo coffee break, catching up with friends, or getting work done with WiFi and outlets available.

They serve great matcha lattes, pastries, and specialty coffee, with GCash, Maya, cards, and cash accepted for easy payments. They also host workshops, like the Flowers & Coffee sessions and Latte Art class, and offer coffee cart bookings for events.

  • Location: Bloq Res. Showroom, GND Arcade, Cebu City
  • Operating Hours:
    7:00 AM – 6:00 PM | Tue–Sun
    10:00 AM - 12:00 MN | Fri-Sat
